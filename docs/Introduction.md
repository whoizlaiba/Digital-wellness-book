Introduction

We live in a world where digital technology is part of almost every moment of our lives. From smartphones and social media to online work and entertainment, screens surround us from morning to night. While technology has made life faster and more connected, it has also created new challenges for our mental health, focus, and overall well-being.

This book, Digital Wellness: Finding Balance in a Tech-Driven World, is designed to help readers understand how technology affects the mind and body. It explains why constant connectivity can lead to stress, distraction, and burnout, and why finding balance is more important than ever. The goal is not to reject technology, but to use it in a healthier and more mindful way.

Through simple explanations and practical ideas, this book guides readers toward building positive digital habits. By learning how to manage screen time, practice mindfulness, and create healthy boundaries, readers can take control of their digital lives and design a future where technology supports well-being instead of harming it.
