# Chapter 3: Building Healthy Digital Habits

Healthy digital habits begin with awareness and intention. Instead of eliminating technology, the focus is on using it wisely. Simple actions such as setting screen-time limits, scheduling device-free moments, and prioritizing meaningful online activities can make a significant difference.

This chapter provides practical guidance on creating routines that support focus and well-being. It explains how to reduce digital distractions, manage social media use, and design daily habits that promote balance. Small changes, when practiced consistently, can transform the way technology fits into everyday life.
